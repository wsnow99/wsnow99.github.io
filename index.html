About Me

I'm a second year master student from School of Aerospace, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) and pursing my master's degree in Mechanical Engineering. I am working on digital twin and learning with Prof. Qi Zhou. Prior to that, I received my bachelor degree from HUST, with a major in Aircraft Design and Engineering.

My research interest inludes data-driven science and engineering, digital twin, physics-informed neural network and fault diagnosis. My ultimate objective is to integrate deep learning algorithms with traditional scientific methodologies to create intelligent agents that bridge the virtual and physical world.
